Lasagna

  • Prep Time:

    1 hr 30 min

  • Dificulty:

    Medium

Making lasagna can be time-consuming, but the results are well worth the wait. You'll find a detailed ingredient list and step-by-step instructions in the recipe below, but let's go over the basics.

Ingradients

  • Meat
  • Garlic
  • Tomato sause
  • Milk
  • Flour
  • Spices of seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses (Parmesan and hard cheese)
  • Butter

How to make lasagna step-by-step

  1. Make the bolognese sause
  2. Make Bechamel sause
  3. Layer lasagna
  4. Cover with cheese
  5. Bake
  6. Let it rest before serving

How to layer lasagna

  1. Grease a backing form with butter
  2. Lay out the lasagna noodles
  3. Add bolognese sause on it
  4. Spread with bechamel sause
  5. Sprinkle with grated hard cheese
  6. Repeat the previous 4 steps 3-4 times (depends on your backing form size)
  7. Lay out last lasagna noodles and cover it with Bechamel sause and parmesan cheese

Bake lasagna for about 50 minutes at a temperature of 180 degrees Celsius.
